CoolMathGames Mini

by CalvinK19

A version of coolmathgames that can actually run on the school chromebooks. To find a game, press CTRL + F and type in the name of the game you want to play- then just click on the title.
Games which say "No Game Assets Available!" are Flash games and cannot be played. This is not a problem on my end, but one with CoolMathGames (I cannot fix it). Also, some games will load correctly but error out while playing (like the game Black and White), so you will also have to go to the official site to play those games.
Lastly, some games also load to the page with ads. Just click the play button and it'll load in fullscreen.
Have fun. Official CoolMathGames site
