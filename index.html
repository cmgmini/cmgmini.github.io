CoolMathGames Mini

by CalvinK19

A version of coolmathgames that can actually run on the school chromebooks. (as of earlier this september, CK-Express blocks ads on coolmathgames.) To find a game, press CTRL + F and type in the name of the game you want to play- then just click on the title. You can also use the search bar provided below. Games which say "No Game Assets Available!" are Flash games and cannot be played. This is not a problem on my end, but one with CoolMathGames (I cannot fix it). Also, some games will load correctly but error out while playing (like the game Black and White), so you will also have to go to the official site to play those games. Lastly, some games also load to the page with ads (like Vex 3). Just click the play button and it'll load in fullscreen. If you still wish to use the official site, drag this --> Remove Ads <-- to your bookmarks bar. Whenever you click on the bookmark, it will remove all ads until you reload or navigate to another page. Have fun. Official CoolMathGames site
